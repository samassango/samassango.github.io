BOMB THREATHS

In case of a bomb scare immediately do the following:

  • Do not panic, keep calm and establish if caller is legitimate or a prank.
  • Keep the caller on the line for as long as possible.
  • Gather as much information as possible.
  • Try to establish where the device is placed.
  • Notify the control room, use the land line.
  • Notify the bomb squad.
  • Do not use the cellphone or the two way radio as it could trigger the bomb.
  • Evacuate the building.
  • Condone of the building and wait bomb squad.
  • Never touch or go near the suspicious device wait for the professionals.
  • Document all the events in the occurrence book

STRIKE ACTION PLAN

It is becoming a regular occurrence in our country these days, especially in our higher learning institutions. In the event of a possible strike, immediately notify the manager on duty. Commence lockdown procedures, prevent strikers form entering the premises. Notify the local S.A.P.S wait for the strike action/ riot unit to be deployed from the company. Notify the client, always maintain calm with the protesters. Allow the SAPS and riot unit to mediate and obtain the list of demands from the protestors. If the protestors become violent, avoid using force. Use of water cannons and rubber bullets is allowed to restrain the protestors. All members of the riot unit and SAPS to ensure protestors do not damage or vandalize the client's property or assets. Document all the incidents in the occurrence book.

CROWD CONTROL TECHNIQUES

Standing in long queues for long hours could lead to frustration among people trying to enter a venue. In the event of heavy traffic flow of people the following should be implemented:

  • Notify the control room to send back up and equipment needed to control the crowd.
  • Erect crowd control barriers to restrict heavy flow of people into venue.
  • Erect razor mash and high security fencing to also restrict the flow of people.
  • Use metal detectors at all points of entries to aid in the searching of people entering the venue.
  • Use dogs to aid in sniffing out drugs, alcohol or any other banned substance from entering the venue
  • Extra security personnel to be deployed to be extra vigilant and aid in easing the flow of people into the venue.
  • Prevent stampedes from occurring.
  • Always maintain calm in the venue.
  • Record all incidents in the occurrence book.

CONTINGENCY PLANS

In the event of power failure the following has to be in place to ensure smooth and uninterrupted power supply.

CONTROL ROOM

  • Immediately inform the manager on standby of the power failure.
  • Drop all switches in the main box.
  • Switch on the backup generator.
  • Always ensure there Is alternate fuel in the generator.
  • Once the generator comes on switch on the uninterrupted power supply
  • Switch off all unnecessary appliances.
  • Notify the electricity department of the power failure.
